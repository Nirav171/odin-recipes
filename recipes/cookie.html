Description:

The macademia nut cookie.Undoubtebly the best cookie filled with the best type of chocolate and has the best crunch of them all!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup sugar (or ½ cup sugar + ¼ cup brown sugar)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped macademia nuts

Steps To Make The Macademia Nut Cookie:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until smooth.
  3. Mix in vanilla extract.
  4. Stir in flour until just combined.
  5. Fold in white chocolate chips and macadamia nuts.
  6. Scoop onto the baking sheet and bake 10–12 minutes, until edges are lightly golden. Cool before serving.
